Introduction

As a senior product designer with over 9 years of experience, I'm focused on creating user-friendly and impactful digital products. My goal is always to design things that truly solve user problems and also bring value to the business. My expertise also extends to DesignOps, where I've successfully planned and implemented various design guidelines and design systems to streamline workflows and ensure design consistency.

For the past three years, I've worked as a Senior Product Designer at Dcard (Taiwan's leading social media platform). My main focus there has been on driving user growth and enhancing the creator ecosystem. My journey into design started differently. When I was a child, I really enjoyed doodling and creating things, but I never thought it would become my career. It was during my college studies in Library and Information Science at National Taiwan University that I discovered user experience (UX) concepts, and they immediately caught my interest. I took courses in psychology and knowledge management, and even got a Knowledge Management Program Certificate. After a short time working as a marketing associate, I realized it wasn't the path for me. That's when I decided to teach myself design. I started by designing Line stickers and campaign pages. My marketing background actually helped a lot, as it gave me a better understanding of users. This, combined with my strong enthusiasm for learning, helped me become a product designer. My commitment to continuous learning also extends to leveraging modern tools like AI for coding, which helps me enhance my design workflow and improve collaboration with developers.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading novels, which helps me find balance. I also love traveling, and Japan is my favorite country. These personal interests help me relax and also give me new perspectives on people and different cultures.
